# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in the Federal Grant Compliance Framework project a harassment-free, professional experience for everyone. This project exists to serve nonprofit organizations and the communities they support.

## Expected Behavior

### Positive Actions üëç

- Be respectful of different opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the nonprofits we serve
- Demonstrate empathy and kindness
- Share knowledge generously
- Assume good intentions
- Maintain confidentiality when discussing specific organizational challenges
- Provide accurate regulatory information and cite sources

### Unacceptable Behavior ‚ùå

- Harassment of any kind
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sharing confidential organizational information without authorization
- Intentionally providing misleading regulatory information
- Spam or inappropriate commercial promotion
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional workplace

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including:
- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Email communications
- Any public representation of the project

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Email:** damoah.dao@gmail.com

**What to include:**
- Description of what happened
- Date and time
- Where it occurred (GitHub issue #, discussion, etc.)
- Any relevant screenshots or links

All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
1. **Warning** - Private clarification about the violation
2. **Temporary restrictions** - Suspension from participation for a specified period
3. **Permanent ban** - Permanent removal from all project spaces

The response will be proportional to the severity and pattern of behavior.

## Special Considerations

### Professional Standards
This project serves a professional compliance context. All participants should:
- Maintain workplace-appropriate behavior
- Respect the sensitive nature of compliance and audit issues
- Avoid sharing organizational information that could cause harm
- Recognize the legal and regulatory implications of advice given

### Regulatory Accuracy
Given the compliance focus:
- Provide accurate information based on current regulations
- Cite regulatory sources (e.g., "per 2 CFR 200.403")
- Indicate uncertainty rather than guessing
- Defer to subject matter experts when appropriate

### Confidentiality
When discussing implementation:
- Do not identify specific organizations without permission
- Focus on general lessons rather than specific cases
- Remember that audit findings and compliance issues are sensitive
